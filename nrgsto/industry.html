Energy storage presents unique challenges. Building from the ground up, Custard tailors your solutions to your needs.

In a new industry...

  • Few candidates offer the tangible experience that would indicate success in the role
  • Potential clients are unfamiliar with the technology, skeptical of the projections and hesitant to proceed
  • Our energy storage and training experience allows us to prepare industry outsiders for success in this role
  • We nurture relationships with key strategic client contacts so your sales team can benefit from reference calls & case studies

With high client expectations...

  • Savings projections tend to be optimistic, creating expectations that ignore realistic operating constraints
  • Your clients consist of experienced B2B executives representing sophisticated organisations with the resources and motivation to hold you accountable for performance
  • High performance requires a perfect interpretation of utility tariffs and other market pressures so the asset can dispatch accordingly
  • A brief outage or issue can wipe out an entire month of the system’s economic productivity
  • Over the course of a ten-year contract or longer, battery degradation constitutes a growing obstacle to reaching the original performance projections
  • We understand your original sales projections, the revenue streams they rely on, their operating assumptions and the limits of their forecast
  • We offer your clients the exact mix of responsiveness, technical precision and transparency they need
  • We analyze system performance for concerned customers so we can flag actual unresolved issues, or educate your clients on what is in fact effective system operation
  • We bring your clients hope and patience by keeping their attention on larger annual trends and reminding them of upcoming improvements on your product roadmap
  • We offer your engineers crisp and efficient technical feedback when our team identifies a new bug or other issue

Unwieldy logistics...

  • DR program participation and incentive compliance typically requires a series of administrative tasks outside your clients’ scope of expertise
  • Planned maintenance and unplanned outage resolution both require technicians to visit your clients’ work sites for potentially disruptive activities
  • Without a side-by-side comparison between the utility’s meter and your own, it’s not possible to assess the system’s results with precision or offer the highest performance
  • Utilities frequently modify their tariffs, which can materially affect each system’s optimal dispatch strategy
  • We collaborate with your experts to identify the exact administrative needs of each project
  • We solicit your clients for every signature, authorization, contract amendment or utility data required
  • We support your technicians by coordinating site access in a way that respects your clients’ security and other protocols
  • We help you design and manage a database and processes which automatically detect metering data and tariff discrepancies

And long timelines...

  • More than a year can go by from the time a contract is signed to the time a system goes operational
  • Long delays often cause staff turnover with key contacts, or a reduction in customer engagement, both of which can threaten or delay future phases of the project
  • The end of deployment is only the beginning of a 10-year relationship or more, during which customer expectations will have to be continually met
  • Over the course of deployment, we keep your clients involved with regular updates
  • Your sales team, project managers and engineers can remain focused on their important and expensive tasks
  • Once deployment is completed, we continue our regular cadence of customer interactions with your key accounts
  • All your clients benefit from same-day email replies and immediate phone support
Custard will be there through all phases of your project lifecycle, and help you turn industry challenges into opportunities for customer satisfaction.
