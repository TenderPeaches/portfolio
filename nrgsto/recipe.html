Thanks to our extensive experience serving companies who opt-in to the energy storage revolution, we've fine-tuned the recipe for customer satisfaction in our field. Here are the main ingredients.

We create client ambassadors

Energy storage is complex, and it can be challenging to demonstrate its value to skeptical customers. It is crucial to nurture ambassador relationships and educate your key contacts. In turn, these clients can faithfully and confidently represent your energy storage platform to their peers.

We strive for high performance

From a hiring process that identifies strong candidates, through the training and coaching that produces empathetic and industry-aware professionals, Custard sets a high bar in recruitment and in the quality of service that your clients will receive.

We build real trust

In performing a side-by-side comparison of your system’s meter data and that of the utility’s, we’re able to ensure your settlement process is accurate to the penny and demonstrate the level of accountability demanded by your most rigorous and detail-oriented clients.

We deliver total visibility

The energy storage experience is a passive one for your clients, and demonstrating its value requires an explicit effort. System activity and performance must be easily viewed by your clients through web dashboards or detailed reports sent each month.

We provide peace of mind

With a commitment of ten years or more, the day will come that your clients have a new question, an unexpected issue or some performance concerns to discuss. With Custard, this prospect is not daunting because your clients know they can get same-day answers, by phone or email.
