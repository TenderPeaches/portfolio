Customer Operations Specialist

Position Summary

As a Customer Operations Specialist, you will represent our clients in the renewable energy space by responding to the needs of their commercial & industrial customers, and by owning requests & issues as they come in, until they are resolved. You should expect most of the work to relate to battery storage in particular, so it will be difficult to succeed without prior renewables experience or a mind for technical learning.
From case to case, you may face tasks such as understanding battery behavior by looking at data, explaining battery performance to B2B contacts, demonstrating a software dashboard, investigating billing or contractual issues, or soliciting clients for required authorizations. In support of these functions, you will receive industry training from Custard veterans, and familiarize yourself with the commercial applications of battery storage technology and the North American landscape of the industry in general.
This position reports directly to senior decision-makers so there is ample opportunity to provide feedback, make process recommendation, demonstrate initiative with creative analysis or project management, and influence the growth of the role. Successful candidates with a track record of customer satisfaction will eventually earn the opportunity to expand the scope of their assignment with Custard’s clients and take on leadership responsibilities within our customer care team.

Responsibilities

  • Become an expert of our clients’ products, tools and processes
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for any client account assigned to you, answering calls during opening hours (eight-hour window between 8AM to 8PM EST) and responding to emails within 24 hours
  • Respond to all inquiries with professionalism and empathy, owning each request or issue until it is conclusively solved
  • Escalate customer care issues and process gaps to senior leadership
  • Produce documents in support of your customer cases and other customer care projects
  • Participate in customer care projects as required

What we’re looking for

  • At a minimum, outstanding verbal and written communication skills, paired with the empathy to understand the needs of a diverse audience of entry-level employees, managers and executives
  • Ideally, years of demonstrated customer service experience in a B2B contact center, preferably in a technical industry
  • Ability to juggle a quickly evolving set of tasks & continually reassess priorities while paying attention to detail
  • Deep accountability towards customer outcomes; don’t expect to follow a script, the job is to understand what the customer needs and meet those expectations
  • Demonstrated ability to quickly & easily learn new, sometimes complex technical concepts
  • Experienced Office user, Word is a formality, and you know advanced features of Excel and PowerPoint
  • A balance between autonomy and teamwork; you complete work without being micro-managed, but also work easily within a team, communicate transparently and are receptive to feedback
  • Access to an adequate workstation, at home or otherwise
  • Eligible to work in Canada

Job Location

Down the road, in a world without pandemic, the Custard team will occasionally assemble in person, in Montreal, Quebec. We support clients across North America’s time zones and because many of them are based in San Francisco, we occasionally organize business trips to visit them. For now, this is exclusively a work-from-home opportunity for Canadian residents.

What we offer

  • Opportunity to join a skilled, dynamic and ambitious team as we tackle a universal need with modern solutions
  • Compensation proportional to your customer care & renewables experience, ranging from $20 to $26 per hour
  • A closely-managed onboarding process including training for your role and the battery storage industry
  • The ability to strongly influence the nature of your role over time with close proximity to senior leadership
  • A work environment with high standards, honest communication, no ego and a ton of flexibility
  • A workstation setup program to make sure you can be comfortable & productive
If you are interested in this position, please send your CV and a cover letter to jobs@custardcs.com with the Subject line of CUSTOPS-10122 Job Application.

Renewables Programs Operator

Position Summary

In the world of renewable energy in California, many projects are subsidized in part by government and utility programs. As a Programs Operator, your core function is to perform all the tasks required to be compliant with these programs. This includes the completion of documents, the review of electrical interval data, timely submittals to program administrators, and the collection of signatures from our clients’ customers. Consider California’s Self-Generation Incentive Program (SGIP) a good example of one of these programs.
Because our team operates across several markets, a successful candidate would need to show some familiarity with the language of renewable energy and an ability to learn new technical concepts quickly. Necessarily, tangible experience with energy storage, interval data and government subsidies are all key assets for any candidate. A demonstrated ability to handle a large, detail-oriented and perpetually shifting workload is also crucial to the role.

Responsibilities

  • Using Salesforce reports, monitor the status and upcoming deadlines of all customer projects participating in the Programs under your scope
  • Generate forms required by each Program, populating them accurately using data available to you in Salesforce
  • Solicit B2B customers to have these forms signed by executive stakeholders
  • Access, analyze and format spreadsheets of electrical interval data according to Program compliance rules
  • Submit forms and other documents to Program administrators, ensuring each one is received prior to any applicable deadline
  • Coordinating with Customer Operations and other colleagues as necessary when issues are identified
  • Support the team with related projects as needed

What we’re looking for

  • Ideally, years of experience in the renewable energy sector; at least a familiarity with the language of energy
  • Demonstrated ability to master new technical concepts quickly
  • Integrity and great attention to detail to produce work that is precisely correct
  • Tangible project management experience or organizational skills to oversee hundreds of timelines
  • Professionalism paired with excellent interpersonal skills, outstanding verbal and written communication
  • A balance between autonomy and teamwork; you complete work without being micro-managed, but you easily work within a team, communicate transparently and are receptive to feedback
  • Tangible, prior experience with the SGIP program or the California market is not required but very valuable
  • Expert user of Excel, at least very comfortable with the rest of the Office suite
  • Prior Salesforce experience an asset
  • Access to an adequate workstation, at home or otherwise
  • Eligible to work in Canada

Job Location

Down the road, in a world without pandemic, the Custard team will occasionally assemble in person, in Montreal, Quebec. We support clients across North America’s time zones and because many of them are based in San Francisco, we occasionally organize business trips to visit them. For now, this is exclusively a work-from-home opportunity for Canadian residents.

What we offer

  • Opportunity to join a skilled, dynamic and ambitious team as we tackle a universal need with modern solutions
  • Compensation proportional to your industry and transferable experience, ranging from $22 to $28 per hour
  • A closely-managed onboarding process including training for your role and the battery storage industry
  • The ability to strongly influence the nature of your role over time with close proximity to senior leadership
  • A work environment with high standards, honest communication, no ego and a ton of flexibility

Sales Executive

Position Summary

Custard’s core mission is the satisfaction of our clients’ customers. While their customers consist of commercial and industrial companies across all industries, our clients all operate in the renewable energy space, with a particular focus on battery storage operation.
The Sales Executive we seek utilizes superior communication skills, industry familiarity and impeccable work ethic to uncover and solicit the battery storage and solar developers who could benefit from a partnership with Custard. With a deep understanding of industry language, our clients’ challenging recruitment landscape and the suite of Custard services, you will engage decision-makers to propose solutions tailored to their needs, negotiating as needed until an agreement is reached.
Custard is looking for an assertive, self-driven candidate who can understand our market and demonstrate ownership of Custard’s growth objectives. Reporting directly to senior leadership, successful Sales Executive will eventually earn the opportunity to lead Custard’s larger expansion strategy.

Responsibilities

  • Master Custard’s customer care and other staffing solutions
  • Develop a strategy to identify members of the renewable energy community who may benefit from our services
  • Reach out to prospective clients, discover their needs and propose the appropriate Custard solution, negotiating as needed
  • Involve senior leadership in late contractual stages to support & finalize your proposed terms
  • Negotiate with prospective clients as needed to reach
  • Maintain a sufficient proficiency with battery storage to understand the needs of our clients
  • Track all opportunities and customer interactions in Salesforce

What we’re looking for

  • Years of successful experience with the sales skillset, including market research, cold outreach, consultative selling and contract negotiation
  • Years of B2B corporate experience, ideally related to prior Sales experience
  • Ideally, prior experience in the renewable energy sector; at least a demonstrated ability for fast technical learning
  • Confident & empathetic communicator who can actively listen, read an audience, identify needs, respond to objections, communicate professionally & compellingly, verbally and in writing
  • Self-starter with ample autonomy & discipline to drive towards objectives without constant supervision
  • Data entry experience and rigor to track all sales opportunities & conversations on Salesforce
  • Access to an adequate workstation, at home or otherwise
  • Eligible to work in Canada

Job Location

Down the road, in a world without pandemic, the Custard team will occasionally assemble in person, in Montreal, Quebec. We support clients across North America’s time zones and because many of them are based in San Francisco, we occasionally organize business trips to visit them. For now, this is exclusively a work-from-home opportunity for Canadian residents.

What we offer

  • Opportunity to join a skilled, dynamic and ambitious team as we tackle a universal need with modern solutions
  • Compensation of $20 per hour plus competitive and straightforward commission structure
  • A closely-managed onboarding process including training for your role and the battery storage industry
  • The ability to strongly influence the nature of your role over time with close proximity to senior leadership
  • A work environment with high standards, honest communication, no ego and a ton of flexibility+
  • Opportunity to become Custard’s senior sales leader, contingent on performance & leadership
