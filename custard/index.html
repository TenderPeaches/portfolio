Custard

Who we are

We're the people who solve your most important customer issues.

Which customers?

With a proven track record supporting businesses in energy, telecommunications, banking and hospitality, Custard is an industry-agnostic specialist of all things customer experience. Whatever your sector, product or technology, we can help.

What issues?

If you already know what obstacles stand in the way of your vision for an uncompromising customer experience, we're here to deliver the people, process and technology solutions you need. If you're not sure why customers aren't as satisfied as they should be, we'd love to find out on your behalf. Get in touch with us and find out how we can help.

What we do

Staffing

With direct access to the large, cost-effective and bilingual pool of talented customer care agents in the greater Montreal area, Custard’s rigorous selection & training process produces professional and empathetic specialists of your specific customer base, at a fraction of your local hiring costs.

Training

Resting on core principles of adult learning, our team is trained to demonstrate such subject matter expertise as to be indifferentiable from your own. Whether because you don’t operate a full-time training division or to supplement your existing training activities, these detailed, insightful, and engaging training experiences are available to your employees and customers alike.

Customer Service QA

“Trust but verify” is a popular philosophy, but time-consuming and sometimes challenging to execute. Custard develops a custom framework to assess the performance of all your customer-facing teams and individuals. Then, our auditors operate the program to provide consistent, clear, and actionable employee feedback, or train your team members to operate the program themselves.

Lifecycle Management

There’s no more direct path to customer satisfaction than from the mouth of customers themselves. Collecting actionable customer feedback, however, requires a precise application of technology, communications and process. Custard develops and operates the framework by which the voices of your customers directs us to your most urgent problems.

Business / Gap Analysis

By dissecting product performance data, customer data, business processes and communication practices, our business analysts explore & master the ecosystem of your organization. Good practices are highlighted, but we focus on locating the source of any dysfunction. Ultimately, Custard provides succinct, actionable conclusions supported by data, experience, and true subject matter expertise.

Project Management

Whether you already know which problem needs to be solved, or because of our prior business analysis, our project managers stand ready to execute on agreed-upon strategies. When designing an elegant process, implementing a more coherent product change, forming a new team, or launching a new product, Custard’s experience is at your disposal to ensure the desired customer impact right from the start.

Where our projects need further support in these areas, we’ll be happy to recommend trusted experts from the Custard network.
