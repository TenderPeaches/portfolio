Welcome to Pattitude!

We offer personalized canine activity and home petsitting in the center of Montreal and surroundings (at adjusted rates, details here). We are fully covered by an insurance policy (certificate available on demand).

Contact us to schedule a free consultation.

Dog Walking

  • Private walks at no additional cost - we walk your dogs alone, allowing us to give them our full attention
  • Reinforcing your dog's good walking habits through modern behavior training
  • Guaranteed stimulation for dogs with energy to spare
  • Prices starting from $20 (tax included) per walk
  • Save 10% for any bill of 5 or more walks

Pet Sitting

  • Hourly rates from $20/h (tax included), only 30 minutes minimum
  • Overnight/Walks package available, includes three walks per day for complete coverage in case of lengthy departures
  • No additional fees for extra animals, save for dogs
  • We gladly take care of cats, birds, reptiles, fishes, plants and others

Order your services online, by email or by phone at 514-220-0686

We offer our regular services from 8:00AM to 6:00PM, Monday through Friday. We are available at a premium outside of these hours and into the weekend. Our overnight stays begin at 10:00PM, ends at 6:00AM the next day and includes a short walk before and after bedtime.
